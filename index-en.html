Data Analysis Projects

Cohort Analysis – SuperStore

Using Excel, I developed a customer retention analysis (Cohort Analysis) with real data from a national supermarket chain. From data cleaning and structuring, I identified the best acquisition groups and behavior patterns, generating strategic insights for retention and sales.

  • Tools: Excel, VLOOKUP, Pivot Tables, Conditional Formatting
  • Result: May/2014 cohort had the best retention after 6 months (11%)
  • Key Insight: Promotional campaigns positively impact repurchase behavior
  • View Project

RFM Analysis – Customer Segmentation

I classified customers based on their purchase behavior using the RFM technique (Recency, Frequency, and Monetary). The analysis enables customer base segmentation to create personalized campaigns and maximize marketing returns.

  • Tools: Excel (INDEX, MATCH, SUMIF, PERCENTILE)
  • Segments created: Champions, Loyal, Promising, Lost, and more
  • Results: 40% of customers are potential loyal ones; only 1% "Do Not Lose"
  • View Project
